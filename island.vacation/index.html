Welcome to Mykonos island

Mykonos is one of the most beautiful islands of Greece and one of the most famous in the world for the blue sea and nightlife.

You can find everything that you want! Traditional food or expensive restaurants. Don't forget eat souvlaki!

Is very close to Athens, about 4 hours by the boat from Piraeus or just 30 minutes by aeroplane.

Scorpios, Cavo Paradiso, Mykonos bar, Caprice are just a few of the bars that you can drink coctail until the sunrise.
